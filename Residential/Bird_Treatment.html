Bird Treatment


No matter where you live, sometimes birds can be a pest rather than a pleasure. Large flock of birds can play havoc with homes and businesses in a variety of ways. So, it’s important that we are able to control birds when their behaviour becomes a nuisance. Bird waste being acidic, the problem of spreading various diseases and terminal illness has risen gradually.

We provide the following services to stop the bird from entering the vicinity and covers the potential nesting areas, leaving the birds unharmed.

Treatment:

Netting Solutions: This pest control policy of bird management is based on covering the possible point of entry of the bird and prevents nesting in nooks, corners, and cracks.
Bird Spyke System: In this process, upward pointed spikes prevent the bird from landing on various platforms.
Bird Wire: Stretched wire attached to a spring creates a non-hostile and unstable platform for the birds to land.

It can be difficult to find one bird control method that completely solves the problem. It often takes a combination of methods to get things under control. Because different species carry various diseases, it is not advisable to try and handle the problem yourself. Additionally, not all methods will be suitable for every situation, so it’s best to consult a professional who will be able to offer expert advice and the benefit of years of experience.


The team at GoGreen Hygiene Services specialises in bird proofing in a humane way wherever possible – we focus on prevention rather than eradication
